Corporate Finance

Corporate finance is an area of finance that deals with sources of funding, the capital structure of corporations, the actions that managers take to increase the value of the firm to the shareholders, and the tools and analysis used to allocate financial resources. We help you with your finacial planning & management.

Corporate finance is primarily concerned with maximizing shareholder value through long-term and short-term financial planning and the implementation of various strategies. We help you do that with a division or department set up to oversee the financial activities in Nepal.

Strategic financial management
